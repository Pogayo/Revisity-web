Overview

About the course

The mathematics foundation of computing module is designed to teach students the foundational concepts in mathematics that will support them throughout their computer science degree programme and beyond. As mathematics is reflected in almost every endeavor in life, the module is also designed to enable students to apply its knowledge in solving real world problems.

Upon completion, students shall be able to:

  • Understand fundamentals of calculus and algebra
  • Use matrices and matrix manipulation operations to solve relevant real world problems
  • Prove and disapprove mathematics arguments using several techniques learned
  • Describe and solve real world problems in terms of differential equations
